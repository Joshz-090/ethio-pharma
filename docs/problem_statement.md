# Problem Statement: Pharmaceutical Access in Arba Minch

## 🏥 The Context
In Arba Minch, Ethiopia, patients face a fragmented and inefficient pharmaceutical landscape. Critical medicines are often out of stock at primary health centers, forcing patients to wander from one private pharmacy to another.

## ⚠️ Key Challenges
1.  **Search Inefficiency**: Patients spend hours traveling physically between pharmacies to find specific medications.
2.  **Inventory Fragmentation**: Private pharmacies have no unified way to showcase their available stock to the public.
3.  **Prescription Vulnerability**: Lack of digital validation leads to errors or delays in fulfilling sensitive prescriptions.
4.  **Reservation Uncertainty**: Even if a medicine is found via phone, there is no guarantee it will be held until the patient arrives.

## 🎯 The Goal
To create a localized digital ecosystem that centralizes medicine availability, allows secure reservations, and empowers pharmacies with real-time demand data.
